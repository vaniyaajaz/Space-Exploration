02 Meet Your Crew

What is the perfect crew for a space mission?
Click below to find out exactly who will accompany you
on your next space destination

Commander

Reid Wiseman

A US Navy veteran with 27 years of experience and a former Flight Engineer on the ISS.
He brings the high-level executive decision-making needed for deep-space autonomous operations.

Pilot

Victor Glover

The first person of colour assigned to a lunar mission. He previously piloted SpaceX
Crew-1 and holds three master's degrees, specializing in flight systems and engineering.

Mission Specialist

Christina Koch

The first woman assigned to a lunar mission. She holds the record for the longest single
spaceflight by a woman (328 days) and has conducted six spacewalks, providing unmatched
long-duration endurance expertise.

Mission Specialist

Jeremy Hansen

A Canadian Space Agency (CSA) astronaut and former fighter pilot. He was the first
Canadian to lead an entire NASA astronaut training class, making him an expert in team
cohesion and international diplomacy.

Endurance Reserve

Peggy Whitson

Now a commercial astronaut with Axiom Space, she holds the US record for most
cumulative days in space (over 675 days). She is the gold standard for maintaining a spacecraft over
years of use.
